About The Game

Hacim Adventure

Hacim Adventure was inspired by one of the very first computer games ever played by the founder of OdotNYC and Hacim Alyn. As a kid discovering computers and digital worlds for the first time, the game that stood out the most was the classic puzzle adventure Chip’s Challenge. The creativity, problem solving, keys, doors, hidden paths, and maze like gameplay left a lasting impact that would later inspire a new generation of ideas.

Years later, that inspiration evolved into Hacim Adventure, a modern retro style browser game built with its own identity, themes, and story world. The project blends puzzle solving, exploration, technology, and retro gaming culture into one experience designed to feel nostalgic while introducing something fresh for a new audience.

The name Hacim carries an even deeper meaning. It is the founder’s son’s first name, Micah, spelled backwards. The game represents legacy, creativity, family, imagination, and the passing of inspiration from one generation to the next through technology and gaming culture.

Built under the Hacim Alyn creative universe and powered by RoboTechi Co., Hacim Adventure is both a tribute to classic computer gaming and a step toward building original interactive worlds for the future.
